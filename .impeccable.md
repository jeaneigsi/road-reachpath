## Design Context

### Users

Business development and sales teams, recruiters, advisors and investors who
need to understand a professional target and choose a legitimate route to an
introduction. They use the product during focused research sessions and need
confidence in the provenance of every claim.

### Brand Personality

Trustworthy, incisive, discreet. ReachPath should feel like a calm research
desk for consequential conversations, not an automated outreach machine.

### Aesthetic Direction

Provisional direction: editorial intelligence workspace. Light warm paper
background, deep ink typography, muted copper action accent, and restrained
navy secondary surfaces. Asymmetric editorial composition, generous whitespace,
serif display typography paired with a practical sans-serif body. No gradients,
neon, glassmorphism or decorative dashboard charts.

### Design Principles

1. Evidence before excitement: show provenance, confidence and limits near the
   decision they support.
2. One decisive next action per view; secondary actions stay quiet.
3. Progressive disclosure: start with target and objective, reveal graph and
   strategy detail after the run is ready.
4. Human control: generated outreach is always editable and never sent
   automatically.
5. Dense information must remain calm, scannable and keyboard accessible.
